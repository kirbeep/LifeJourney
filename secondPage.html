I went to Goodwill Elementary School from 2013 to 2019, and those years are packed with memories I'll always treasure. That's where my academic journey began, and where I was shaped into a sharp-minded student who brought pride to the school. My teachers taught me not just lessons from books, but also the value of learning itself—and the importance of respecting the kind-hearted educators who never stopped sharing their wisdom.

My elementary years hold a special place in my heart. That time was full of joy and unforgettable experiences that the younger version of me truly lived to the fullest. The friends I made along the way made those years even more meaningful.

I'm incredibly thankful for the teachers, club advisers, classmates, and friends I met in that little school. Even now, I still picture every corner of Goodwill Elementary—because every part of it holds something special for me.
