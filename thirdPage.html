In Junior High, I went to San Bartolome High School. During my first few days there, I couldn't help but miss my elementary years, starting fresh in a new school felt like stepping into a more challenging, yet exciting chapter of my life.

San Bartolome HS holds a special place in my heart too. Being in a bigger school opened up so many opportunities: I got to join clubs, try new things, and meet amazing people who eventually became close friends.

The four years I spent there were truly unforgettable. The experiences and lessons I gained helped me grow in ways I never expected. Giving me new skills, deeper understanding, and shaping me into the person I am today. And the friends I made along the way made everything better. School was always more fun with them by my side, and those memories will stay with me forever.

After my years at San Bartolome High School, a new beginning unfolded at STI College Novaliches, where I stepped into senior high school.
